## Response to Critical Finding #1: RPE1 Odf2-KO preserves cilia

> **Reviewer:** "Odf2-KO in RPE1 does NOT abolish cilia. Viol 2020 PhD thesis (ZMBH Heidelberg): cilium formation preserved, DAs present, SDAs lost."

**Response: FULLY ACCEPTED.**

This finding fundamentally changes the experimental design. We have revised CONCEPT from v2.0 to v3.0 with the following changes:

| v2.0 (obsolete) | v3.0 (revised) |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Paradigm: cilia-dependent | Paradigm: **SDA-dependent** |
| Rescue: tubacin (HDAC6i) | Rescue: **ODF2-FL transfection** (genetic rescue) |
| Tubacin role: rescue | Tubacin role: **negative control** |
| Assumption: KO→no cilia | **Pilot 0 added** — direct verification |

**New central hypothesis:** "Subdistal appendages (SDAs), formed by Odf2/Cep128/Centriolin axis, are CAUSAL for daughter cell size asymmetry through asymmetric microtubule organisation. Cilia are preserved in Odf2-KO RPE1 and are NOT the mediator."

---

## Response to Critical Finding #2: Tubacin inappropriate as rescue

> **Reviewer:** "Tubacin does not restore SDAs/Odf2. Rescue should be ODF2-FL, not tubacin."

**Response: FULLY ACCEPTED.**

- ✅ **KO+ODF2-FL** added as primary genetic rescue (gold standard)
- ✅ **KO+tubacin** retained as NEGATIVE control (confirms SDA-dependence, not cilium-dependence)
- ✅ **Cep128-KO** added as orthogonal SDA disruption
